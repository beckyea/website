Mini-Minecraft

Overview

This was the final project for CIS560: Introduction to Computer Graphics and I worked on it with Isa Jiminez and Ben Leitner. The goal of the project was to create an interactive miniature version of Minecraft. I was in charge of handling the player physics, game state and engine updates, player swimming, multi-threading of terrain generation, creation of sandbox mode, and procedurally generated post-process shaders for weather.
