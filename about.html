About Me

My name is Becky and I'm a rising senior at the University of Pennsylvania, majoring in Mechanical Engineering & Applied Mechanics and minoring in Computer Science. I am also submatriculating into a Masters in Robotics, and will graduate in December 2018. I'm interested in software for mechanical systems, particularly IoT and robotics.

Though I began taking computer science courses in high school, I began playing with code (mostly HTML) in middle school. After making a few websites for both myself and for school groups, I put an on the iOS App Store during my senior year of high school. I also found an interest in mechanical design, and was on the build team for my high school's FIRST Robotics team. At Penn, I spent 3 semesters doing layout for the food magazine, Penn Appetit, and was a computer science TA for 5 semesters. I am also the Webmaster for Penn's branch of ASME.

Those who know me would say that I am a "people person." I have served as social and recruitment chair, as well as membership education director for my sorority, Alpha Omega Epsilon. In my free time, I enjoy following the San Francisco Giants and Golden State Warriors, traveling, adventuring, running, and playing with my dog.
