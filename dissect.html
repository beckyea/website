Dissect: Model of Rotary Telephone

This was a project for MEAM 101/510, Introduction to Mechanical Design, and I worked on it with Anna Estep and Nikhil Chari. We purchased an old rotary phone on Ebay and took it apart to model all of the internal mechanical components, as well as the outer shell. I was responsible for the majority of the interior components. In the original model (modified slightly for interactivity below), the connected components are mated to function together.
