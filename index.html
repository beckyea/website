Welcome

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio site! I'm a Robotics Masters student at Penn, going into my final semester and planning to graduate this coming December. This site has been a long-term work in progress - with a barebones beginning of design work through my pure software development phase to my discovery of the intersection of the two fields and current interest in a mechatronics, focusing on software for robotic systems in motion - and is half portfolio, half sandbox for template-free web dev tinkering (appologies if things break!). Feel free to browse and learn more about me as well as the mechanical, robotics, and coding projects that I've worked on.

In addition to the work shown on this site, I've also done non-disclosable work at X (formerly Google X) and Amazon; more information can be found on my resume.

I'm currently looking for full-time employment starting in early 2019, so if you want to reach me, feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn or by sending me an email at rabram@seas.upenn.edu.
